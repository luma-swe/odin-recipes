Crêpes

A French classic!

Ingredients:

  • 250g of all-purpose flour
  • a sprinkle of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp of oil
  • 1 tbsp of sugar
  • 500l of milk
  • 35g of butter

Steps

  1. Add the flour in a big bowl.
  2. Add the salt
  3. Dig a little well in the middle then add the eggs in it.
  4. Add the sugar and the oil to the eggs then mix.
  5. Pour the milk little by little and whisk.
  6. Melt the butter in a saucepan.
  7. Add the cool butter to the batter.
  8. Let it rest for minimum 30 minutes.
  9. Grease a skillet.
  10. Once hot enough, pour a ladle of the batter to the skillet.
  11. Cook each side 2 minutes.
  12. Cover the ready crêpes with a cloth.
  13. Bon Appetit! You can serve them with jam, chocolate or sugar.
