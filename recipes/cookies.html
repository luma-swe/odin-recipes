Cookies

Easy and fast snacks for kids!

Ingredients:

  • 85g of soft butter
  • 1 egg
  • vanilla sugar
  • 85g of sugar
  • 150g of flour
  • 1 tsp of baking powder
  • salt
  • 100g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200° C.
  2. Dice the butter into small cubes.
  3. Add the egg.
  4. Add the sugar, vanilla sugar, flour, baking powder and salt to the mix. .
  5. Mix to form a homegenous dough.
  6. Add the chocolate chips.
  7. On an oven tray, spread a parchement.
  8. Form little cookie balls with your hands.
  9. Make sure to leave enough space between the cookies as they will grow in the oven.
  10. Bake them for 20 minutes.
  11. Voilà!
