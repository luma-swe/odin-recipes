Tiramisu

Making the famous Italian dessert has never been easier!

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 2 tbsp of sugar
  • 500g of mascarpone
  • Savoiardi
  • Coffee (to sprinkle on top)

Steps

  1. Seperate the egg yolks from the egg whites.
  2. Add the sugar to the yolks and whisk.
  3. In a seperate bowl, whip up the egg whites.
  4. Incorporates the whipped up egg whites to the sugar yolks mix.
  5. Add the mascarpone to the mix.
  6. Mix gently until obtaining a smooth batter.
  7. In a plat, pour some water and add a tsp of coffee.
  8. Dip the Savoiardi in the water then dispose it in a baking mold.
  9. Repeat the process until layering the base.
  10. Add the batter on top of the Savoiardi layer.
  11. Repeat the process twice.
  12. Sprinkle the top with coffee
  13. Place the mold in the fridge and let it sit for 7 hours.
